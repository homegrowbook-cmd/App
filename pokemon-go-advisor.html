💡 Notes
CP Calculator: Base stats for all Pokémon can be found on PVPoke or GO Hub. Tip: Select a Pokémon in IV Rank – the base stats will be automatically transferred to the CP Calculator.

IV Rank: Rank 1 = 15/15/15 is rarely optimal! For Great League, 0/15/15 or similar is often better, as a lower Attack IV allows a Pokémon to be played under the CP cap at a higher level. The rank is based on the stat product at optimal level.

Shadow: +20% more damage dealt, +20% more damage received. Great for offense-heavy Pokémon.
Purified: All IVs boosted by +1 (max 15/15/15), 10% cheaper to Power Up, gets Return.
