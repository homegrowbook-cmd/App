ℹ️ About IV Ranks

Rank #1 = the single best possible IV spread for a given league (maximises stat product).

Stat Product = Attack × Defense × Stamina (all at the best level under the CP cap). Higher = more bulk and damage.

Great League ≤ 1500 CP  |  Ultra League ≤ 2500 CP  |  Master League no cap (Level 40).

A top-100 spread (rank ≤ 100 out of 4096) is excellent for PvP. Top-500 is good. Top-1000 is acceptable.

IVs are determined by appraising your Pokémon in-game (3-bar appraisal system).
